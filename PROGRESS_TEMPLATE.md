# My Progress — Intern Onboarding

> **How to use this template:** Copy this file to `MY_PROGRESS.md` (in the repo root) on
> your first day. Fill it in as you go — it's your personal journal through the program.
> Future-you (and anyone who reviews your work) will thank you. See
> [LEARNING_PATH.md](LEARNING_PATH.md) for the overall plan and
> [PROGRAM_CHECKLIST.md](PROGRAM_CHECKLIST.md) for the high-level checklist.
>
> **Why keep this?** Writing things down is how you actually learn. When you hit the same
> bug in 3 months, your notes save you an hour. When you're asked "what did you do?", you
> have a record. When you feel stuck, re-reading your wins reminds you how far you've come.
